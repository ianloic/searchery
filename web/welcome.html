Searchery
Web search results in your Firefox 3 awesome bar

Welcome to Searchery. Thank you for trying out this extension. A few things have changed, here's a quick rundown of what has happened and where to go from here.

Your address bar now contains a search icon that you can use to manipulate your search settings.

Click on the icon to show the Searchery menu. From this menu you can configure what shows up in your awesome bar results window, configure which search engines you have installed and show or hide the old Firefox search box.

When you start using the address bar you'll see search engines and search results show up where they didn't before. Since all these different search engines are right at your finger tips you'll probably want to manage them using the Manage search engines... menu item.
