Searchery
Web search results in your Firefox 3 awesome bar

Searchery is a new Firefox extension which brings web search into Firefox 3's new Awesome Bar. As you type into your address bar Firefox will search your history for pages that match and Searchery will search the web.

Searchery moves the functionality of the Firefox search box into the address bar. This means there's just one place to type what you're looking for and it frees up more room on your screen to see results. All your OpenSearch web search engines are available with just a couple of clicks.

Web search results from Google.com and Amazon.com are available next to your history and bookmark results. So if you're looking for a site you've been to before it's easy to spot, if you're looking for something new it's right there too.

Your search results are split into sections. You can move betwen them by pressing page-up or page-down or just by using your mouse. Take a look at my instructions page or the support page at Get Satisfaction.

Install Searchery

Searchery is built on top of new features only available in Firefox 3. To try it out you'll have to upgrade to Firefox 3 first.

Searchery is a Firefox extension. It depends on features that are unique to Firefox. There are no plans to port it to other web browsers. Why don't you download Firefox?
